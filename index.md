# CallMeGus Privacy Notice

Last updated: July 20, 2026

CallMeGus is a private application used for Amazon advertising reporting, analytics, and campaign optimization.

## Information Accessed

CallMeGus may access advertising-related information made available through Amazon APIs, including campaign performance, advertising metrics, campaign settings, targeting information, and related account data.

CallMeGus is not intended to collect or process Amazon customer personally identifiable information.

## How Information Is Used

Information accessed through Amazon APIs is used only for:

* advertising reporting and analysis;
* campaign performance monitoring;
* campaign optimization and recommendation generation;
* automation of authorized advertising management activities.

## Data Sharing

Amazon advertising information is not sold.

Information may only be used by authorized users or service providers involved in managing the applicable Amazon advertising account.

## Data Security

API credentials and authentication tokens are stored securely and are not intentionally exposed in public repositories or shared publicly.

Access to Amazon information is limited to authorized purposes related to reporting, analytics, and advertising management.

## Data Retention

Information is retained only as necessary for reporting, analysis, historical performance comparison, and authorized advertising management purposes.
